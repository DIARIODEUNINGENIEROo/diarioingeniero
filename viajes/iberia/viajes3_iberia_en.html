3. Travel through the South of Spain, Jaén and Granada

August 28 to 31, 2021

On this trip I visited some of the cities with the greatest architectural, artistic and cultural heritage in Spain. These are the cities of Jaén, Úbeda and Baeza, which gained importance from the 15th century. You would see some charming towns in the province of Jaén such as Martos and Alcalá La Real, which are born on a mountain with great views from its top.
Lastly, he would spend a couple of days in the magnificent city of Granada that cost so much to recover from the Moors to the northern towns of the peninsula. I would visit the Alhambra mainly, the Albaicín neighborhood and the cathedral area at night.
